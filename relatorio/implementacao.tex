\section{Implementação}

Para a implementação, só é preciso computar os filtros:

\begin{align}
\dot{\lambda} &= N\lambda + u \\
\dot{\eta} &= N\eta + y
\end{align}

Temos ainda que $\alpha =
\hat{\rho}(-c_1z_1-d_1d_1-\xi-\bar{\omega}^\intercal\hat{\theta})$. Logo:
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial \alpha_1}{\partial y} &= -\hat{\rho}(c_1 + d_1) +
\hat{\rho}e_2^\intercal\hat{\theta} + N\\
\frac{\partial \alpha_1}{\partial \eta} &= e_2^\intercal N^2 \hat{\rho} \\
\frac{\partial \alpha_1}{\partial y_r} &= \hat{\rho}(c_1 + d_1)\\
\frac{\partial \alpha_1}{\partial \hat{\theta}} &= - \rho\bar{\omega} \\
\frac{\partial \alpha_1}{\partial \hat{\rho}} &= -(c_1+d_1)(y-y_r) +
e_2^\intercal N\eta -\bar{\omega}^\intercal \hat{\theta} + Ny
\end{align}
